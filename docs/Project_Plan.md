# 🚀 Project Plan – Dental Clinic Management System

# 📌 Overview

The Dental Clinic Management System is a comprehensive web application designed to automate and simplify clinic operations, including patient management, appointment scheduling, treatment workflows, financial management, reporting, and administrative activities.

The project follows a modular monolithic architecture to ensure maintainability, scalability, and long-term extensibility.

---

# 🧭 Project Phases

| Phase | Title | Description |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 1️⃣ | Planning & Preparation | Requirements, documentation, applications, tools, architecture |
| 2️⃣ | Foundation & Infrastructure | Project setup, applications, models, infrastructure |

# 🗂 Applications

## core

Shared components and project infrastructure.

## accounts

Authentication, authorization, users, roles and permissions.

## patients

Patient profiles, documents, medical history and records.

## doctors

Doctor profiles, specialties, working schedules and attendance.

## appointments

Appointment scheduling, calendar, waiting list and reminders.

## treatments

Treatment records, dental chart, prescriptions and procedures.

## finance

Invoices, payments, expenses, installments and financial reports.

## inventory

Medical supplies and inventory management.

## notifications

SMS, Email and scheduled notification services.

## reports

Business reports and export services.

## dashboard

Administrative dashboards and analytics.

## settings

Clinic configuration and system settings.
